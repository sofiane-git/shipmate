# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/),
version 2.1.

## Our pledge
We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education,
socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment: empathy, respect for
differing opinions, graceful acceptance of constructive feedback. Unacceptable behavior:
harassment, trolling, insults, publishing others' private information.

## Enforcement
Report unacceptable behavior privately to the maintainer via GitHub. All complaints will be
reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

The full text and enforcement guidelines are at the link above.
